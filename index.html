WhatsApp Direct vs WhatsApp Connect

Two ways CleverTap gets your messages onto WhatsApp. The difference decides billing, setup speed, and which accounts each one suits.

WhatsApp DirectWhatsApp Connect

Which one to use

Larger, regulated businesses (telco, insurance, banking) often land on Connect, because they usually already hold a BSP relationship, a verified number, and negotiated Meta rates they will not move. Direct is the cleaner choice for smaller, faster-moving teams where turning WhatsApp on quickly, without chasing a separate BSP contract, is the real advantage. Matching the flavour to your stage is a good early question to settle.

Who wires it up

The connection and provisioning itself is a technical setup task, typically handled by a messaging or technical team, not the people evaluating use cases. This tab is here so you can gauge feasibility, not so you have to do the plumbing.

Template Categories & the Economics That Shape Every Prototype

Meta runs four categories, per-message pricing since 1 July 2025. The category and the free-window rules decide the cost of every use case, so they are tagged on every card.

Good to know · CleverTap limitation

CleverTap does not support Authentication templates. Through CleverTap WhatsApp Direct you can send Marketing and Utility templates, plus free-form Service replies inside the 24h window. OTP and verification codes over WhatsApp are not deliverable through CleverTap Direct. The Marketing "Product Messages" sub-type is also not supported. So if you need one-time passwords over WhatsApp, that would run through a dedicated auth provider, SMS, or a separate BSP, not CleverTap. The 5 Authentication cards in the library are flagged and shown for completeness only.

The two free windows every rep should recite

  • 24-hour service window. When a user messages a business, a 24h window opens for free service replies, and it resets with each new user message. This is why inbound-first mechanics (click-to-WhatsApp, QR, "reply YES") are so cost-efficient.
  • 72-hour entry-point window. When a customer messages you from an Ad that Clicks to WhatsApp or a Facebook Page CTA, all your messages for the next 72 hours are free, including templates.
Auth-international markets, if auth is routed elsewhere

Since CleverTap Direct cannot send Authentication templates at all, this only matters if the customer routes OTP through a separate provider. If they do, note that Meta charges higher authentication-international rates for OTPs sent to a set of markets (the list expanded in Feb 2025 and includes markets such as Egypt, Malaysia, Nigeria, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, and the UAE). An SMS-beating cost comparison into those markets is worth checking against the current rate card first.

Rates move

As of 1 Jan 2026 Meta adjusted regional rates (India marketing up ~10%, India moved to INR billing) and reviews rates roughly quarterly. Any cost model in a deck needs a "rates current as of, confirm the live card" footnote.

Template types available in CleverTap

  • Basic, Limited-Time-Offer with expiry timer (not allowed in Utility), Carousel up to 10 cards.
  • WhatsApp Flows for in-chat forms (feedback, reservations, data capture), quick-reply and CTA buttons, marketing-opt-out button.
  • Category constraint: only Marketing and Utility are usable. Authentication and Marketing (Product Messages) templates are not supported in CleverTap.
  • API constraint: the Create-Campaign API does not yet support Carousel, LTO, or copy-code templates. Those are dashboard-built only. Flag it honestly.

Can They Run More Than One WABA? Yes. Here Is How.

Routine, with limits worth knowing. The structure is a nesting doll: Meta Business Portfolio holds WABAs, WABAs hold phone numbers.

LevelLimitNotes
Business PortfolioUp to 20 WABAs after Business VerificationRequest more via a Meta support ticket.

Templates per WABAUp to 250Per WABA, not per number.

Setup path in plain terms

  • Business creates or uses a Meta Business Portfolio, completes Business Verification.
  • Then either Meta's Embedded Signup flow (fastest, generates the WABA and provisions the number) or, for Connect, shares the WABA with the BSP as a partner (up to two partners) via Meta Business Suite.
  • For Direct, CleverTap runs that provisioning inside Settings > Channels.
Two hard constraints to plan around

A WABA belongs to only one business portfolio and cannot be migrated between businesses. Its time zone and currency cannot be edited once a credit line is attached. So a multi-market business (one running across many countries) must decide its WABA and number architecture up front, because it cannot be cleanly re-parented later.

Confirmed for CleverTap

CleverTap supports multiple WhatsApp numbers or WABAs under one dashboard, to scale across geographies, brands, or functions. The nuance to confirm for a specific rollout: each CleverTap project maps to a distinct number, though a single WABA can be referenced across projects. Get the project-to-number mapping confirmed before committing to a multi-market architecture.

Replying to Conversations

Yes, two-way works. This is what makes the service-window and two-way cards in the library deliverable.

  • When a user replies to one of your messages, CleverTap's Conversations feature lets you reply directly from the dashboard, inside the 24h service window.
  • Subscribing to CleverTap BSP (WhatsApp Direct) gives access to Campaigns, Journeys, and Conversations. So keyword auto-replies, quick-reply payload capture, and agent handoff are all in scope.
  • Replies inside the 24h window are free-form (no template needed) and free of Meta charge. This is the cost-efficient heart of any inbound-first mechanic.
Is the Service category fine? Yes

Service is not a template you create or submit for approval. It is the free-form reply layer inside the 24h window, and CleverTap's Conversations feature fully supports it: free-form text and file replies, ticket statuses (New, Active, and so on), autoresponders, FAQs, and quick replies, all available with the WhatsApp add-on. The one thing to remember is you cannot initiate with a Service message. It only exists as a reply. So Service sits under your two-way and free-window cards as the reply layer, never as a standalone campaign.

Template Creation Process

On Direct you build templates inside CleverTap. No need to create them in Meta first.

StepWhat happens
1 · Create in CleverTapSettings > Channels > WhatsApp > WhatsApp Direct, pick the WABA, Templates tab, + Template. CleverTap BSP customers create templates directly on the platform, no Meta-first, no double entry.
2 · Pick categoryMarketing or Utility only. Authentication and Marketing (Product Messages) are not selectable. LTO templates are Direct-only and Marketing-only.
3 · Name and languageTemplate name in lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores only. Select one or more languages.
4 · Build the bodyHeader (text, image, video, document, location), body with variables, and buttons: up to 5 custom buttons plus 1 marketing opt-out button. CTA types are Call Phone and Visit Website; Quick Reply supports custom payloads for response tracking.
5 · Template Library (optional)Direct offers out-of-the-box template structures. + Template > Template Library pre-populates the structure, category, and components.
6 · Submit for reviewSent to Meta. Approval can take up to 24 hours. Status auto-syncs from Meta into CleverTap, with manual refresh available.
Two escape hatches

If creating a template in CleverTap fails, build it in Meta WhatsApp Manager and import it. On Connect, you register or import your BSP's already-approved templates into CleverTap (Import Template works only if the BSP supports import and shares the endpoint and credentials).
